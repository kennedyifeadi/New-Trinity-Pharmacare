About Us

Founded in 2023, Trinity Pharmacare Limited is a reputable pharmaceutical company based in the vibrant city of Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. We have quickly become a trusted name in the industry, specializing in the wholesale, distribution, and marketing of pharmaceutical products and medical supplies for pharmacies, hospitals, and other healthcare providers.

Our extensive portfolio includes a wide range of branded and generic pharmaceuticals, OTC items, surgical supplies, medical devices, and equipment.
